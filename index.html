About Ndjar

N'djar is an integrated system (digital platform) associated with the Agricultural Land Suitability Assessment system that uses technological channels for the dissemination and distribution of various agricultural information and statistics for the efficient and appropriate use of the national productive force of soils and lands. It is based on the interpretation of soil vocation, environmental characteristics, and the assessment of land limitations for agricultural use. The platform aims to provide the most suitable use possibilities for a given area, considering different alternatives for management practices. The name "N'djar" has a local significance, as it means "Farmer" in the Pepel dialect of Guinea-Bissau, which highlights the platform's focus on supporting the agricultural community.

Ndjar Impact

  • Better decision-making: The platform can assist farmers and agricultural groups in making educated decisions regarding the use of land, the choice of crops, and management techniques, which will increase output and efficiency.
  • Sustainable farming: The platform can support environmentally friendly farming methods that protect soil quality, biodiversity, and natural resources by disseminating knowledge about environmental variables and constraints.
  • Climate change adaptation: The platform can assist farmers in adopting adaptive measures to improve their resilience against climate-related risks by taking into consideration environmental variables and climate change impacts.
  • Gender equality: By providing equal access to agricultural information and resources, the platform can contribute to reducing gender disparities in the agricultural sector and empower women farmers.
